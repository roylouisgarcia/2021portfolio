About Me

Roy Louis Garcia

.. believes that ...

  • learning is perpetual
  • technology is not neutral
  • a developer has the responsibility not only to hide complexities from technology users, but also to deliver products that are easy to use, in excellent and assured condition, and able to provide positive impact to the society
  • accessibility of technology should be inclusive and not viewed as a problem to be fixed but as a potential to be explored

... actively participates in virtual communities of subject matter experts, influencers and passionate advocates of various applications of technology in:

  • lifelong learning
  • mobile computing
  • computer security
  • assistive technology
  • cyberethics

... explores the overlaps of technical knowledge, social media marketing, analytics, machine learning, data science, and big data.

... is interested in ubiquitous computing(UbiCom), and Internet of Things (IoT), augmented reality(AR) and virtual reality(VR), since society benefits from the multiple gadgets built around us, like mobile phones, tablets, voice assistants, wearable technologies, IoT appliances and sensors, software applications, APIs, IFTTT, cloud computing, workflows, data, ideas, etc..

... is a technology manager, a meticulous programmer, conscienscious administrator, a problem solver, and a perpetual learner.
